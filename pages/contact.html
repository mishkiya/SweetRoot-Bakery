Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing orders at least 72 hours in advance for standard cakes. For custom designs or wedding cakes, we suggest 4-6 weeks notice to ensure availability and proper planning.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We offer a selection of gluten-free and vegan cakes. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when placing your order so we can accommodate your needs.

What are your bakery hours?

Our bakery is open Monday through Saturday from 2:00 LT to 2:00 PM LT. We're closed on Sundays. Pre-orders can be picked up during these hours.

Do you deliver?

We offer delivery within a 15-mile radius for orders over 3000Birr. Delivery fee is 500Birr. For weddings and large events, we can discuss special delivery arrangements.

Can I customize a cake from your menu?

Absolutely! While we have standard menu items, we're happy to customize flavors, fillings, and designs to make your cake perfect for your occasion.
